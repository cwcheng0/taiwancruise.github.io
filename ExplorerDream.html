Explorer Dream Cruise

Your journey to new horizons in Asia Pacific, Australia & New Zealand

Ship Info Dining Entertainment Amenities

Basic Ship Information

Ship Name: Explorer Dream

Cruise Line: Dream Cruises (formerly part of the fleet)

Gross Tonnage: 75,338 GT

Guest Capacity (Lower Berths): Approximately 1,850 - 1,852

Total Cabins: 928 - 929

Year Launched/Refurbished: Launched 1999, Extensively Refurbished April 2019

Key Features: Features the exclusive "The Palace" luxury private enclave with suite accommodation and butler service.

Cruising Regions: Known for exploring Asia Pacific, including destinations like Japan, Hong Kong, Vietnam, as well as Australia and New Zealand.

Restaurants & Dining

Enjoy a wide array of international and Asian cuisine across 15 restaurants and bars.

  • Main Dining (Complimentary): Offers the main dining room experience with varied menus.
  • Lido Buffet: The international buffet for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
  • Pavilion: A complimentary Chinese restaurant (A la carte or small buffet).
  • Specialty Dining (Extra Charge):
    • Seafood Grill by Mark Best: Alfresco dining by the acclaimed Australian chef, focusing on seafood and grilled meats.
    • Mozzarella Ristorante & Pizzeria: Italian dishes with a Japanese twist.
    • Blue Lagoon: 24-hour hawker-style Asian specialties like noodle soups and rice dishes.
    • Silk Road Chinese Restaurant: Elegant, fine-dining Chinese cuisine.
    • Umi Uma: Japanese restaurant featuring sushi, sashimi, noodles, tempura, and Teppanyaki.
    • Hot Pot: Traditional hot pot dining experience.
  • Cafes & Bars: Lobby Cafe (for specialty coffee and pastries), Little Cake Cafe, The Dream Boutiques (for champagne/whiskey lounges).

Entertainment & Activities

There's never a dull moment with world-class shows and diverse activities for all ages.

  • Zodiac Theatre: Home to large-scale production shows like the Korean non-verbal performance, **Cookin' NANTA**, and acrobatic shows like **Harmony**.
  • Parthenon Pool Deck: Roman-themed pool area featuring **Caesar's Slide**, hot tubs, a stage for live bands, and an LED screen.
  • The Zone & ESC Experience Lab: Area for virtual reality games and other interactive entertainment.
  • Sportsplex: Facility for games like football and basketball.
  • Nightlife: Dance under the stars at the onboard nightclub, enjoy live music, or experience a **Silent Disco**.
  • Kids' Programs: **Little Dreamers Club** with games, workshops, and costume parties for children.
  • Wellness & Shopping: Crystal Life Spa (one of the largest at sea), Gym, Jogging Track, and **The Dream Boutiques** for luxury retail.

Onboard Amenities

  • The Palace: An exclusive luxury enclave with private suites, pool, restaurant, and butler service.
  • Staterooms: Impressive array of spacious staterooms, many with private balconies.
  • Casino: Resorts World At Sea.
  • Other Services: Reception, Shore Excursions/Cruise Consultant, Clinic, Photo Corner.
