疫情下的台灣跳島郵輪

In 2020, COVID-19 spreads to the world. Many countries and cruise companies announced no plan for the cruises. However, Taiwan started the first island-hopping cruise ship tours: Explorer Dream, docked at the Port of Keelung, and 538 crew members underwent a 14-day quarantine in hotels in Taiwan.

Explorer Dream

The Explorer Dream carries at most 1,800 people, with all passengers in sea-view rooms.

Independant air-cons

All rooms are sea-view based rooms.

Food and beverages

All served by crew members with masks and gloves.
