MSC Bellissima

A Modern Marvel at Sea

Basic Ship Information

Class: Meraviglia-class
Gross Tonnage: 171,598 GT
Guests (Max): Up to 5,686
Length: 316 meters (1,037 ft)
Decks: 18 (15 accessible to guests)
Staterooms: 2,244

Key Feature: Home to the stunning Galleria Bellissima, a 314-foot-long Mediterranean-style promenade covered by a spectacular 262-foot LED Sky Screen.

Restaurants & Dining

MSC Bellissima offers 10 restaurants, including four main dining rooms and a variety of specialty options.

Included Dining

Main Dining Rooms (Posidonia, The Lighthouse, Il Ciliegio, Le Cerisier)

  • Offer a daily changing menu of Mediterranean and International specialties.
  • Open for Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

Marketplace Buffet (Deck 15)

  • Vast, open-plan buffet with numerous stations, including a glass-fronted mozzarella-making area.
  • Open for most of the day, offering a casual dining experience.

Specialty Dining (Additional Charge)

Butcher's Cut

Stylish American-style steakhouse featuring superb prime cuts of beef.

Kaito Teppanyaki & Sushi Bar

Authentic Japanese cuisine with entertaining Teppanyaki chefs and fresh sushi/sashimi.

L'Atelier Bistrot

A charming French bistro offering savory snacks, charcuterie, and classic French dishes.

HOLA! Tapas Bar

A casual tapas bar curated by a renowned Spanish chef, Ramón Freixa.

Entertainment & Activities

The ship boasts a wide array of activities for all ages, from world-class shows to high-tech games.

Shows & Venues

Carousel Productions at Sea (Carousel Lounge)

Exclusive original shows combining acrobatics, dance, and live music, created especially for MSC's Meraviglia-class ships.

The London Theatre

A Broadway-style theatre hosting a fabulous program of international production shows.

Galleria Bellissima

The main promenade that transforms at night with music, parties, and the spectacular LED Sky Screen shows.

Fun & Games

Polar Aquapark

One of the most intricate water parks at sea, featuring three twisting slides, multiple pools, and the exhilarating Himalayan Bridge rope course.

Sportsplex

Large multi-purpose arena for sports and games during the day, which becomes a dance/party venue at night.

Virtual Reality & Arcade

High-tech experiences including an F1 Simulator, a 4D Cinema, and a full-size Bowling Alley (additional charge).

Kids & Teens Clubs

Dedicated areas and programs for all ages, including partnerships with LEGO® and Chicco®.
