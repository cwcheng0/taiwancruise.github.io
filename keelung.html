基隆港靠港旅遊 Keelung Harbour Travel

主題:港口旅遊 Subject: Harbour Travel

基隆港旅遊有2條路線： 基隆市跟台北市

In Keelung harbour travel, there are 2 trips: One is to stay in Keelung and the other is to Taipei.

交通:
-可搭火車到台北市
-可搭公車1813到台北市
需要:悠遊卡/一卡通
景點:都在台北市

Traffic:
-Train to Taipei
-Bus#1813 to Taipei
You need a IC card.
Most tourists go to Taipei city.

Hiking: Tamsui Kavalan Historical and Cultural Trails, north trail
淡蘭古道北路跟中路的起點位於基隆暖暖，我們的步行起點，從暖暖的暖東峽谷出發，結束時也在暖東峽谷。對於企業包團，行程可結合附近地方共2個景點。 Culture Tours: Tamsui Kavalan Historical and Cultural Trails(North and Middle) starts from Nuannuan, Keelung. We start our trail from Nuandong Valley. If you book our tour, we can arrange another place to have a one day tour.

早上我們集合後，就搭乘旅遊巴士到暖東峽谷。在這裡開始我們的步行，加上沿途拍照預計總時間約2~3小時，午餐時間約1.5小時。 中午在大菁休閒農場用餐。 We get tother in the morning. Then we take a bus to the Nuandong Valley. Including the light meal, the total trip is about 3 ~ 4.5 hours. We have a lunch in the Strobilanthes Cusia Farm.
飯後將遊客們帶回基隆，市區旅遊後上船。 After the meal we take tourists back to Keelung, we may have a short trip in Keelung before boarding.
請注意腳步,特別是拍照時;可以的話可以帶登山杖.
Please watch your step when taking a picture. You may carry a stick.

廁所使用：入口處有廁所，結束的地方遊客中心也有一處,需步行上下樓梯。
